Consistency is more important than volume. Aim for a schedule you can maintain, whether that's weekly or several times per month.

Can I write about different topics?

Yes. Many creators cover multiple subjects, though building expertise in a particular niche can help readers know what to expect.

Is CreatorHub suitable for experienced creators?
